Crash Logs Data

When you use our Games and Services, we may collect crash logs and other data related to technical issues and performance. This information helps us understand and resolve issues that occur during gameplay and improve the overall user experience. The crash logs data we collect includes the following:

  • Crash Logs: Detailed reports of any crashes or errors that occur while you are using our Games and Services. These logs may include information about your device, operating system, game version, and the specific circumstances under which the crash occurred.
  • Analytics: Data on how you interact with our Games and Services, such as gameplay statistics, in-game events, and usage patterns. This information helps us analyze and understand player behavior, optimize game performance, and enhance user satisfaction.

Purpose of Collecting Crash Logs Data

The primary purposes of collecting crash logs and analytics data are as follows:

  • Issue Resolution: To identify, diagnose, and fix bugs, crashes, and other technical issues that may arise during gameplay. This helps us ensure that our Games and Services run smoothly and reliably.

  • Performance Optimization: To analyze the performance of our Games and Services and make necessary improvements. This includes optimizing load times, reducing crashes, and enhancing the overall gaming experience.

  • User Experience Enhancement: To understand how players interact with our Games and Services, allowing us to make informed decisions about game design, features, and updates that enhance user satisfaction.

  • Security and Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent security threats, cheating, and other malicious activities that may compromise the integrity of our Games and Services.

How We Use Crash Logs Data

We use crash logs and analytics data in the following ways:

  • Technical Support: To provide effective customer support by quickly identifying and addressing technical issues reported by players.
  • Development and Testing: To inform our development and testing processes, ensuring that new updates and features are thoroughly vetted and free of critical issues.
  • Continuous Improvement: To continuously improve the quality and performance of our Games and Services based on real-world usage data and feedback.
  • Reporting and Insights: To generate reports and insights that help us understand trends, usage patterns, and areas for improvement.

By collecting and analyzing crash logs and analytics data, we aim to deliver a more reliable, enjoyable, and secure gaming experience for all our players. If you have any questions or concerns about our data collection practices, please contact us at qiu1930cs@sina.com.
